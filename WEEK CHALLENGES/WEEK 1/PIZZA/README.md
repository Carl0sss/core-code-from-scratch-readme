# How to make pizza?
```
1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (218°C).
2. Roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface to the desired thickness.
3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving about 1/2 inch of space around the edges.
4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
5. Add your desired toppings (e.g. pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, etc.).
6. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
7. Remove the pizza from the oven using oven mitts.
8. Slice the pizza into desired portions and serve hot.
```
